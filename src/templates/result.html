Gastrointestinal tumors can be classified into microsatellite instable (MSI) or microsatellite stable (MSS) tumors. Microsatellite instability is a DNA predisposition to mutation. In contrast to MSS tumors, MSI gastrointestinal tumor patients are likely to respond to immunotherapy. Using a convolutional neural network, this app classifies gastrointestinal tumors in MSI tumor or MSS tumors from the analysis of images of biopsies staining with hematoxylin and eosin.
